Welcome to the first edition of our Procurement Newsletter.

This first edition gives an overview of some of the key focus areas of the Procurement team across 2020.

We hope that it provides you with an insight into the on-going value that is being delivered through the strong partnerships that exist between our Procurement teams and our Business Units across the globe.

1. Procurement Policy Re-launch

The Procurement community are pleased to announced the launch of our Glanbia Global Procurement Policy. In 2019 we undertook an extensive review of our existing Procurement Policy and made changes to reflect the evolution of our businesses and specifically our procurement processes and systems. Easily accessible links on the OurGlanbia web site will mean that all employees can quickly and easily find the relevant procurement information they require.

Click here to read more.

2. Covid 19 Procurement Action

Since the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic procurement teams across Glanbia had been working hard to maintain supply chains and secure the critical items required to keep our businesses operating safely. In March it was decided to mobilise a Glanbia-wide procurement group to ensure that critical items could be sourced globally if appropriate, allow sharing / leverage of the global supply base in the event of supply constraints and to give an overview of critical stock levels.

Click here to read more.

3. SAP Procured Project

Glanbia is transitioning to a fully electronic ordering and invoicing model using SAP Ariba as its principal system for transacting with suppliers for both direct and indirect spend. Glanbia has over 5000 Ariba enabled suppliers transacting successfully on the Ariba network. This phase of the project is predominantly focusing on transitioning the direct spend supplies (SAP Procured) to Ariba across the globe.
