Glanbia Digital Media
becomes Glanbia Digital+


Created in 2011, Glanbia Digital Media began life as a single person hired to support Glanbia navigate social media policy. Over the years, business needs have grown and we’ve changed to best support it.

Adding more people, adding new skills and adding more value.

The name Glanbia Digital Media no longer represents the team so we decided to change to show that we do much more. So say hello to Glanbia Digital+, a new name for your awarding-winning full service internal digital agency.
